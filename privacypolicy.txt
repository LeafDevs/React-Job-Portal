Privacy Policy for School Guidance Department Job Portal

Last updated: 2024-09-20

1. Introduction

The School Guidance Department Job Portal ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children under the age of 13. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information from children under 13 years of age, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2. Information We Collect

We collect limited personal information from children under 13, which may include:
- First name and last initial
- Age
- School name
- Parent or guardian's email address

We do not collect more detailed personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.

3. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used to:
- Provide and improve our job portal services
- Communicate with parents or guardians about their child's use of the service
- Ensure the safety and security of our users
- Comply with applicable laws and regulations

Additionally, we use the collected data to train artificial intelligence (AI) systems. This helps us improve our services and develop new features that benefit our users.

4. Parental Rights and Consent

In compliance with COPPA, we require verifiable parental consent before collecting, using, or disclosing personal information from children under 13. Parents or guardians have the right to:
- Review their child's personal information
- Request deletion of their child's personal information
- Refuse further collection or use of their child's information

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@schooljobportal.edu.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the personal information we collect, with special attention to data from users under 18.

6. Third-Party Access

We do not share personal information collected from children under 13 with third parties, except as necessary to provide our service or as required by law. We do not allow third-party companies to collect personal information from users under 18 for advertising or marketing purposes.

7. AI Training

The data we collect, including data from users under 13, is used to train AI systems. This process is designed to improve our services and is conducted in a manner that prioritizes user privacy and data protection. No personally identifiable information is used in the AI training process.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify parents and obtain new parental consent if we change the way we collect, use, or disclose children's personal information.
